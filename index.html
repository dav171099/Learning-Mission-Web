MISSION STATEMENT

Learning Mission was founded immediately after the end of the 2020 Artsakh War, and is an educational nonprofit organization dedicated to providing an IT education to young veterans of the2020 Artsakh War. Learning Mission ensures access to high speed internet and provides sufficiently powerful laptops in order to optimize the remote learning experience.
